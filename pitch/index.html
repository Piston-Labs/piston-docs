The Problem

Vehicle maintenance is broken for everyone

  • Shops lack systems to retain customers
    They lose 70% of first-time visitors
  • Drivers have no ownership of their maintenance data
    Service history is scattered across shops, gloveboxes, and memory
  • Fleet maintenance is time-based, not usage-based
    Oil changes at 90 days regardless of 500 or 5,000 miles driven
myodo

Why Existing Solutions Fail

Time-based reminders don't match how people drive

  • Shop CRMs guess using calendars
    "It's been 6 months" - but how many miles?
  • OEM apps are brand-locked and dealer-first
    Your Honda app doesn't know about your independent mechanic
  • Fleet tools track location, not service readiness
    GPS says where the truck is, not that it needs brakes
The core issue: No one tracks actual mileage except the odometer itself - and that data stays trapped in the dashboard.
myodo

The Solution

A mileage-based intelligence layer for maintenance

  • Real-world driving behavior replaces calendar guesswork
  • A permanent service record that stays with the vehicle, not the shop
  • Clear visibility into what's coming next, before maintenance becomes urgent
The difference: We know you drove 4,200 miles since your last oil change - not just that it's been 3 months.
