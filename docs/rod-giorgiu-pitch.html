1. Opening

Rod, thanks for taking the time to meet with me.

I grew up in a shop. I remember when AllData came on discs. I've been a mechanic for 13 years and I've owned a repair shop for over half my adult life.

I'm building the product I wish existed.

2. The Problem

Independent shops are losing the retention war.

  • 60% of customers never return after first visit
  • The relationship breaks after the visit - no connection between appointments
  • Calendar-based reminders feel like spam and get ignored
  • Dealerships dominate with OnStar, Toyota Connected, etc.

3. The Solution

Mileage-based reminders that keep customers connected to their shop.

  1. Piston Device - OBD-II telemetry, tracks real mileage ($150)
  2. Cloud Database - Stores mileage + service history, VIN-linked
  3. Shop Dashboard - Runs automatically, no CRM work required

Result: "You've driven 4,800 miles since your last oil change. Schedule with your shop?" One tap. Booked.

4. The Key Insight

"Accuracy builds trust. Calendar reminders feel like spam. Mileage reminders feel helpful."

Who sends the reminder stays top-of-mind. We make sure it's the independent shop, not the dealer.
