myodo Product Guide

Your car. Your records. Your choice.

myodo helps you stay on top of vehicle maintenance without the hassle. Know when service is actually due based on how you drive, keep all your records in one place, and connect with repair shops on your terms.

The myodo Device

What It Does

The myodo device plugs into your car and tracks your actual mileage. That's it. No complicated setup, no monthly data plans to manage, no tracking your location.

It takes about 30 seconds to install - just plug it into the port under your dashboard (every car made after 1996 has one). Once it's in, you can forget about it. The device syncs with your myodo app automatically.

Why Mileage Matters

Most service reminders are based on time: "It's been 6 months, maybe get an oil change?" But that's a guess. If you drive 2,000 miles a month, you need service more often than someone who drives 500.

myodo knows your actual miles. So instead of guessing, you get reminders when service is actually due - not too early, not too late.

Privacy By Design

Here's what myodo does differently: we don't store your location.

The device uses GPS to calculate distance, then immediately discards the location data. We know you drove 47 miles today. We don't know where you went.

Why? Because we don't need to know. And because your driving habits are nobody's business but yours.

For Repair Shops

The Problem We Solve

Independent repair shops lose 60% of customers after the first visit. Not because of bad service - because there's no easy way to stay connected. The customer drives away, forgets about you, and ends up at the dealer or wherever's most convenient next time.

How myodo Helps

When your customers use myodo, you stay connected automatically:

  1. Customer visits your shop
  2. You upload the repair order (drag and drop, takes 10 seconds)
  3. Time passes, customer drives
  4. myodo detects service is due
  5. Customer gets a reminder: "Schedule with [Your Shop]?"
  6. Customer taps to book
  7. You get the appointment

No marketing campaigns. No email lists. No CRM workflows. The system runs in the background while you focus on fixing cars.
